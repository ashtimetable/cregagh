Cregagh (Formerly Ashfield) Detachment

Who are we?

We are Cregagh Detachment. Apart of the Army Cadet Force. We are in H Company in the 2nd (NI) Battalion. We help people who want to join the army or want to do something in their spare time fill their needs. We offer many different experiences too, anywhere from marching to shooting!

Where are we?

We are stationed at Cregagh Presbyterian Church. If you type that into your favourite maps app you will see exactly where we are! If that did not work. you can search up the following: 102-104 Cregagh Rd, Belfast BT6 9ER.

When is Cadets?

Our Parade Nights are always on a Monday from 19:00 (7PM) to 21:00 (9PM). We also host some weekends with the whole of our area where we go down to ballykinler for a couple of days and do training there. Once a cadet progresses through the ranks there will be more of these weekends!.
